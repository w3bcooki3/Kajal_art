ACRYLIC

Acrylic painting, technique in which pigments are mixed with hot, liquid wax. After all of the colours have been applied to the painting surface, a heating element is passed over them until the individual brush or spatula marks fuse into a uniform film.

A LITTLE INTRO

Hey, I'm Kajal — an artist who can't stick to just one medium (and honestly, why should I?). From messy brushstrokes to moody textures, I make things that feel like they've got a little soul, a little chaos, and a lot of me in them. I don't do graphic design, but give me a canvas, clay, or even a weird scrap of fabric — and I'll turn it into something that talks back.

My Exhibitions

I've shown my work at a few cool galleries, and every time someone stands in front of a piece and goes “hmm…” — I consider that a win. Art is personal, weird, and not always polite — just like me. And I like it that way.

Random Facts

I once painted an entire wall because I ran out of canvas. I name my favorite brushes. I believe tea breaks are sacred. And I think art is best when it doesn't try too hard to be art. Welcome to my little corner of the creative universe.

Why I Make Art

Because it's the one thing that makes sense when nothing else does. Because colors speak louder than words. Because sometimes, splashing paint around feels better than scrolling social media. I make art because it makes me feel alive — and if it makes you feel something too, then that's magic.
